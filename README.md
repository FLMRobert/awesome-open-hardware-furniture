## Projects

* [shareable.net](https://www.shareable.net/20-open-source-furniture-designs/) - 20 Open Source furniture designs
* [Jens Dyvik](http://www.dyvikdesign.com/site/portfolio-jens/open-source) - Furniture and other designs by Jens Dyvik
* [Ronen Kadushin](https://www.ronen-kadushin.com/) - Furniture designs
* [CNC Workstation](https://www.evilmadscientist.com/2012/cnc-workstation/) - CNC workstation cart design
* [Height Adjustable Desk](https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:208636) - Height adjustable desk by Tyler Cooper

## Platforms
* [OpenStructures](https://openstructures.net/) - Open modular construction system
* [ShaperHub](https://hub.shapertools.com/) - Shaper community platform for CNC projects
* [Maslow Community Garden](https://maslowcommunitygarden.org/) - Maslow community platform for CNC projects
* [OpenDesk](https://github.com/timrolls/Opendesk) - Repository with free/libre OpenDesk.cc Designs
* [AtFAB Open Source Furniture](https://filson-rohrbacher.com/work/atfab) - Open Source furniture from Filson & Rohrbacher
* [Hartz IV Moebel](http://hartzivmoebel.de/) - Furniture construction plans from Van Bo Le-Mentzel (free for private use)
